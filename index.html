You chose growth
growth needs, or higher-order needs, include:

Respect needs

The requirement for recognition, consideration, and acceptance of oneself and others.

Self-Actualization needs

The desire for self-fulfillment, personal growth and realization of one's full potential.
Choosing growth again and again without having moments to satisfy your physiological, safety and belongingness needs can be regretful in the long term.
School of Life
You chose safety
Safety needs are part of the deficiency needs, or lower-order needs, which include:

Physiological needs

The basic necessities for survival, such as food, water, shelter, and sleep.

Safety needs

The requirement for protection and security, both physically and emotionally.

Relationship needs

The desire for connection, communication and intimacy with others.
If both the physiological and the safety needs are fairly well gratified, then there will emerge love and affection and belongingness needs, and the whole cycle already described will repeat itself...
Abraham Maslow
